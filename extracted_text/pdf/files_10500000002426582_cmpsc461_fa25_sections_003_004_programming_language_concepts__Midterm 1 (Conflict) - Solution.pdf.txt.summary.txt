This document contains the CMPSC 461 (Fall 2025) Conflict Midterm-I Solutions by Dr. Suman Saha. The closed-book exam comprised 6 main questions and 1 bonus, with a maximum score of 80 points. Covered topics include Regular Expressions, Finite Automata (DFA/NFA, conversion), Context-Free Grammars (CFGs, derivation, regex to CFG), Name, Scope, and Binding (static/dynamic, shallow/deep), and LL(1) parsing/grammar ambiguity.
